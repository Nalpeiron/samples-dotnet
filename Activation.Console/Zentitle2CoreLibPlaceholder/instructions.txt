Download the Zentitle2Core library from https://docs.zentitle.io/developers/the-zentitle2core-library-c++/release-notes 
and unzip the binaries into to this folder keeping the same structure as in the downloaded zip:

- Zentitle2CoreLibPlaceholder
  - Linux_x86_64
    - libZentitle2Core.so
  - MacOS_arm64
    - libZentitle2Core.dylib
  - MacOS_x86_64
    - libZentitle2Core.dylib
  - Windows_x86_64
    - Zentitle2Core.dll
